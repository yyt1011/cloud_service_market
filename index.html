Global cloud service market segments and their leaders, 2019

Source: Kinsta via Synergy Research Group

The global cloud service market has experienced rapid growth last year. The pandemic has pushed many companies to shift their businesses online, which created huge demands for cloud services.

The world public could services is expected to grow 6.3% in 2020 to total $257.9 billion, up from $242.7 billion in 2019, according to Gartner, Inc.

North America is the largest cloud service market and Aisa Pacific is the fastest growing market, according to an industry research with key cloud service providers across the world.
